Analysis of the Grouping of Provinces in Indonesia Based on Health Indicators before and after the pandemic

In this study, the authors want to know the health conditions of the Indonesian people and the grouping of provinces that can be formed based on health aspects between before and after the pandemic.

Crop recommendation system based on environmental data.

Indonesia is an agricultural country with the majority of the Indonesian population being farmers. Climate change will threaten the crop production system resulting in instability of crop yields. The purpose of this research is to create an algorithm that can be used to recommend plants based on environmental data that will have an impact on the stability of crop yields.
